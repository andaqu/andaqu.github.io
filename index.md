Hello! I have recently graduated with a M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from **Stockholm University**. I am also currently pursuing freestanding courses at **Karolinska University**.  <!-- I am also currently working as a Data Scientist at Sambla Group. -->

My primary interests are in computational social psychology, computational linguistics, and cognitive science -- namely, how can we build computational models to represent and to understand human social behaviour?

For my Masters thesis, I worked in collaboration with **Spotify** to evaluate automatic topic segmentation and text summarisation algorithms. I was advised by [Panagiotis Papapetrou](https://www.su.se/english/profiles/ppapa-1.194080) and [Maria Movin](https://se.linkedin.com/in/mariamovin).

Before my move to Stockholm University, I also completed a B.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence at **University of Malta**. While there, I worked with [Charlie Abela](https://www.um.edu.mt/profile/charlieabela) to study how the extraction of personality from textual content relates with followee connections created between Twitter users.
